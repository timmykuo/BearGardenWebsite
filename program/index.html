Bear Garden Programs

The activities of the programs at Bear Garden may sound similar to those offered by the other preschools, yet we strive to give each one new meaning with the focus of building up each precious child holistically!

Though being soaked in the highly competitive culture of Bay area for more than a decade, I still hold on to the Biblical principle that cultivating godly character is the number one goal for education! When a person truly follows God’s commands, all other areas of his/her life will fall into the right places. Therefore, all of our activities are conducted according to this belief.

Under that belief, we strive to make our graduates ready for kindergarten since day one they enter Bear Garden. The holistic learning includes, but not limited to, the personal hygiene, safety issues, alphabet, shapes, colors, exercising fine/gross motor skills, writing his/her own name, reading step one books, etc. All these are delivered to the children with the understanding that each child is unique and has his/her own pace.
